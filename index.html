About SuperWise

SuperWise was born from a Media Lab 2025 project, created by a team of six passionate developers and designers. Our mission is to revolutionize how students find their thesis supervisors.

We understand the challenges students face when searching for the right supervisor and the overwhelming number of requests supervisors receive. That's why we created an AI-powered matching system that connects students with supervisors based on research interests and expertise.

Our Impact

  • Streamlined supervisor search process
  • AI-powered matching based on research interests
  • Reduced administrative burden for supervisors
  • Better student-supervisor compatibility
